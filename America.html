1) Little America Hotel - Wyoming

About this hotel

Off I-80, this unfussy rural hotel is 39 miles from
Seedskadee National Wildlife Refuge and 60 miles
from Fossil Butte National Monument.

Frilly rooms and suites offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen
TVs and minifridges, plus microwaves, en suite
bathrooms and coffeemakers. Some have sitting areas.

Amenities include a restaurant, an outdoor pool and
play areas, plus coin-operated laundry facilities, a
gas station and a convenience shop. Parking is available.
