About this hotel

Set 1 km from Nelson Mandela Square, this upscale hotel is 12 km from Zoo Lake in Johannesburg. Offering city or garden views, the polished rooms feature flat-screens, minibars and Wi-Fi access, plus tea and coffeemaking equipment. Suites add separate living rooms; upgraded suites offer 4-poster beds and/or bathrooms with whirlpool tubs. Some quarters provide access to a lounge with free continental breakfast and snacks. Room service is available.
