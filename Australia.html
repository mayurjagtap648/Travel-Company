About this hotel

In a Colonial-style corner building, this laid-back
hotel is a 6-minute walk from Freemantle train
station, a 15-minute walk from Bathers Bay beach
and a 17-minute walk from Freemantle Prison, now
a museum.

The modest rooms sleep 1 to 4 people and come
with Wi-Fi (fee). All have shared bathrooms.

Amenities include a casual bar with a pool table,
plus a TV lounge, and a patio with seating. There
are also guest laundry facilities.
