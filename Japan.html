About this hotel

A 6-minute walk from Minami-Senju metro station, this simple budget downtown hotel is 2 km from both Senso-ji temple and the Tokyo National Museum. Compact dorms have bunk beds, TVs, minifridges and Wi-Fi, as well as desks and shared bathrooms. Private rooms are available, and a female-only floor is offered. Amenities include kitchenettes on each floor, plus a main kitchen and a pared-back dining area. There's also a TV lounge, in addition to guest computers, coin-operated laundry facilities, and complimentary hot drinks in the lobby.
