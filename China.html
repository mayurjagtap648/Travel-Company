1) China World Hotel, Beijing

About this hotel

Set in the Central Business District, this luxury hotel in a sleek building is 5 km from both Tiananmen Square and the Forbidden City. Chic rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screens, minibars and marble bathrooms, as well as panoramic city views. Club rooms add access to a lounge serving food and drinks. Suites have living rooms; some provide kitchenettes and whirlpool tubs. Room service is on offer 24/7.
